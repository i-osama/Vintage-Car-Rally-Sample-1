How to start in the rally?

Classic Rallysport is one of the fastest growing and one of the most friendly forms of motorsport in our times. It is gaining immense popularity world wide. Realizing that every person steeping over the threshold into Classic Rallying has his or her own set of characteristics and each vehicle is different in its own way, it would be absolutely impossible to pen a definitive guide when one consider the vast array of parts, personalities and preferences that abound.

Who can enter?

Any team or "crew" of Driver and Navigator can enter any of the GLORY DAYS CLASSIC RALLY events in any type of car. However, we strongly recommend the use of a "classic, historic or vintage" car. Classic cars bring an extra dimension to any rally. However, we realize that enthusiasts who are not familiar with Classic Rallying might wish to give it a try before committing to the purchase of a classic car, or they may just be interested to go for the tour.
